Historical Origins — Soft Arab Power

Antar & Abla draws inspiration from the legendary Arabic epic of Antarah ibn Shaddad and his eternal love for Abla. The story symbolizes courage, dignity, and devotion.

This opera stands as a defining moment in Arabic operatic history, presenting classical Arab storytelling through a modern operatic form.

Abu Dhabi Performances

Presented in Abu Dhabi at Sheikh Zayed Stadium, the opera was staged as a large-scale cultural production blending Arabic opera with modern scenography and immersive visuals.

The performances reinforced Abu Dhabi's role as a global cultural hub.

Impact of Arabic Opera in the Arab World

Arabic opera represents a powerful cultural renaissance, bridging the region's rich poetic and musical heritage with contemporary global art forms.

It elevates the Arabic language through refined musical composition and strengthens cultural diplomacy — and it is a lever for a sustainable Arab economy.

By presenting Arab narratives on major stages, it shapes a modern, confident, and globally resonant Arab cultural voice.

Muhammad Bin Rashid Reward

The opera was honored by His Highness Sheikh Muhammad bin Rashid Al Maktoum, recognizing its cultural significance and contribution to the Arab arts scene.
